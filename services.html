At Plus Your Settlements, our experienced team work with you to help you understand the process to help minimise stress and doubt through effective communication by taking it step by step with you. We can help with...

Residential Property Settlements

Understand that this is one of the most important transactions any one or family can make. Strong communication and having paperwork completed correctly so things can be handled with a minimum of fuss is our focus for our clients whether it be for the family home or investments. We work alongside you all the way.

Commercial Property Settlements

Our team are experienced in commercial matters and have a solid understanding of what is required to ensure a simple and easier settlement. An understanding of GST is important and when it is implemented are essential in commercial transactions. To protect your interests, talk with our team.

Family/Related Transfers

Whether they be properties that are being gifted between parties or family court orders, they can be stressful. With our experience in related party/family matters we try and take all the stress out of the transaction for you by explaining step by step how the process works.

Change of Name

Use our experience to ensure things are right and done properly - let the headaches stay with us.

Deceased Estates

At Plus Your Settlements, we understand that dealing with deceased loved one's affairs can be a challenge and we have extensive experience in this area, having helped many families over time. The trust given to any one individual to deal with an estate is a great responsibility. We will work alongside you to help you work through what needs to be done for all parties and hopefully lessen the potential stress for all involved.

Subdivisions

Having dealt with many developers over the years, we would naturally like to help you with your project needs as well. Lead by our Licensee, who has extensive experience in this area, we can help make things happen for you with effective communication and solid knowledge of what needs to be done. Give us a call to discuss your development needs today on 08 9274 2833.

Title Searches

Always happy to help you with your search needs - just talk with our team and we can arrange for you.
